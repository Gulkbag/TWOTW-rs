The Wrath Of The Whites Manual

This is the manual for the indie rougelike The Wrath Of The Whites in this manual we will be explaining the basic concepts of the game Without spoiling the fun, So let's start!

Controls:

The controls of the game are pretty simple and can be simplified to this:
    Movement
  • Arrow Keys
  • Numpad arrow keys for Up, Down, Left, Right and Home, Pageup, End, etc for diagonal movement
    Actions
  • G for grabbing items
  • I for opening the inventory
  • C for seeing character stats
  • Return/Enter key for going down stairs
Menu system is as basic as possible, press the key that is next to the option/item and it will be used/selected etc..

Gameplay:

You are dropped into a randomly generated dungeon (like most rougelikes) and left to fight, niggers, jews, minorities and whatever else, items are picked up into the inventory and can be used, there's consumable items, usable items, and equippable items, damaging enemies is as easy as walking into them, equippable items add stats to the player like attack power, defnese, etc
